Things worth adding to your model

  • Break-even price is your allotted price (₹1,130) — below this, exercising loses money before tax; the calculator assumes you'd never exercise below this.
  • Perquisite vs capital gains tax: in India, ESOP/SAR gains are usually taxed twice — as a perquisite (salary income, at your slab rate) on the spread at exercise, and again as capital gains (STCG/LTCG) if the share price moves further before you sell. This tool applies one flat rate for simplicity — check with a CA for your exact slab + holding period.
  • Holding period: shares held over 12 months from exercise qualify for LTCG (lower rate) instead of STCG — worth tracking your exercise date separately from your allotment date.
  • Multiple tranches: if you have SARs allotted at different prices/dates, each tranche has its own break-even and tax timeline — this version handles one tranche at a time.
  • CMP live-fetch is best-effort (via a public Yahoo Finance proxy) and can be blocked by ad-blockers, browser CSP, or being offline — the Exercised/Target Price fields are always editable manually as a fallback.
